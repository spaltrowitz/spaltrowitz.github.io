About Me

I'm a product operations leader at GitHub. My job is figuring out where PMs are losing time and building the systems that fix it. Basically, I'm a PM of PMs.

I started doing the same thing outside of work. I've always been the person thinking "wouldn't it be cool if there was an app for this?" And now that AI has made building accessible to anyone with an idea, I'm actually doing it.

This site is where I put my work out into the world. Try my apps, send me feedback, or pitch me something you wish existed. If you've got an idea you want help bringing to life, let's talk.
