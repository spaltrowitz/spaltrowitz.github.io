About Me

I'm a product operations leader at GitHub with 5+ years building systems that turn customer feedback, product signals, and operational data into structured inputs for product decision-making.

I operate at the intersection of product operations and LLM-assisted tooling — designing feedback loops, building Copilot-powered automations, and creating self-serve systems that help product teams move faster. I'm essentially a PM of PMs, building the infrastructure that makes product managers more effective.

That experience taught me something powerful: how to go from "wouldn't it be cool if..." to a working product. So I took those skills into my personal life and started building a suite of apps using AI-assisted development — things I genuinely want to exist in the world.

Every project below started as a real problem I noticed. And every one of them is something I'm actively building, shipping, and iterating on.
